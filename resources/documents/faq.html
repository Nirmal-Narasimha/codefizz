Frequently Asked Questions

Can it run on Linux?

Codefizz can run on Windows, Mac and all distributions of linux. Check your respective repository to see if it is available. You can download the source code and relavent binary packages for Codefizz here.

Can my computer run Codefizz?

Codefizz runs smoothly if your computer atleast has aleast 2 GB RAM, 1.33 GHz and above processor, and 3 GB of free space.

How is Codefizz different from other editors?

Codefizz offers what other editors do, and much more, in one place. It makes your workflow smoother and enables you to enjoy your time writing code.

Is Codefizz free?

Codefizz is, and always will be free. However, if you like our work, you can donate on G‐Pay.

I installed Codefizz. What to do next?

Congrats! Please check out our forums and extensions, where you will find relavent articles to guide you and help you get started. Best of Luck!
